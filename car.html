Home insurance provides financial coverage for damages to your property caused by unforeseen events like natural disasters, fires, or theft. FAQS

How does car insurance work?

Car insurance works by providing financial protection in case of accidents, theft, or damage to your vehicle. You pay a premium to the insurance company, and in return, they cover the costs of repairs or medical expenses.

What factors can affect the cost of car insurance?

Several factors can impact the cost of car insurance, including your driving record, the type of car you drive, your age, where you live, and the coverage options you choose.

Do I need car insurance if I don't drive often?

Yes, it is still important to have car insurance even if you don't drive often. Accidents can happen at any time, and insurance provides protection in case of unexpected events.

What should I do if I am involved in a car accident?

If you are involved in a car accident, make sure everyone is safe, contact the authorities, exchange insurance information with the other party, and document the scene by taking photos if possible.

Can I bundle my car insurance with other insurance policies?

Yes, many insurance companies offer discounts for bundling multiple insurance policies, such as car insurance and homeowner's insurance. This can help you save money on premiums.
